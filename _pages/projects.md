## Inter-Layer Augmentation for Image Classification

**Project for CS 6670 - Computer Vision**  
_Ithaca, NY_ | _Sep 2023 - Dec 2023_

- Developed inter-layer augmentation techniques to enhance image classification and reduce overfitting in CNNs and Vision Transformers. Co-authored a paper on the findings.
- Implemented GridDropout and ChannelDropout to augment intermediate layers, improving regularization without sacrificing critical information. Conducted experiments on CIFAR10 and CIFAR100 datasets.

## MathSearch

**Project of Cornell Data Science**  
_Ithaca, NY_ | _Sep 2022 - Dec 2022_

- Developed a program for searching math formulae in LaTeX within PDF files, integrated into a website for user interaction.
- Utilized Yolov5 to locate formulae and applied VGG final-layer representation to compare the similarity between located formulae and user inputs (converted from LaTeX).

## Tracing Honorifics in Tibetan: How Their Use and Obligatoriness

**Project for LING 4404 - Language Typology**  
_Ithaca, NY_ | _Feb 2024 - May 2024_

- Analyzed the evolution of honorifics in Tibetan from Old Literary Tibetan to Modern Colloquial Tibetan, focusing on linguistic shifts due to socio-political changes.
- Examined the strategies behind honorific usage across different periods, exploring how these reflect social hierarchies in Tibetan culture.
